title: "Typeform"
---

**Pricing model:** Usage based subscriptions (Hybrid)

**Value metrics:** Number of responses

[Typeform](https://www.typeform.com/) is a no-code SaaS platform offering form based tools that assist companies to engage with their audience and grow business. Their [pricing](https://www.typeform.com/pricing/) is based on a set of subscription plans but each plan has a usage limit which is modeled in Togai as [Entitlements](/docs/rate-cards#entitlement-rate-card).

In this video, Sajed and Adhitya configure Typeform's pricing in Togai before the song [The adults are talking](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=ewOPQZZn4SY) plays out.
