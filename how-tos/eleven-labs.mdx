**Pricing model:** Usage based subscriptions (Hybrid)

**Value metrics:** Number of characters, Custom voices


[Eleven Labs](http://elevenlabs.io/) is a  company that specializes in developing natural-sounding speech synthesis and text-to-speech software, using artificial intelligence and deep learning.

ElevenLabs is primarily known for its browser-based, AI-assisted text-to-speech software, Speech Synthesis, which can produce lifelike speech by synthesizing vocal emotion and intonation.

Their [pricing model](https://elevenlabs.io/pricing) is based on various input parameters like books, articles, papers and voiceovers based on which they have a usage based price per unit.
