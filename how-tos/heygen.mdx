**Pricing model:** Usage based subscriptions (Hybrid)

**Value metrics:** Total number of video minutes (as credits)

[HeyGen](https://www.heygen.com/) is a next-gen generative AI video platform with customizable AI avatars, right from your browser. Their [pricing](https://www.heygen.com/pricing) is directly based on consumption of credits where 1 credit = 1 minute video. You can see in the below video, Sajed and Adhitya configuring their pricing model in Togai before the song [Last Christmas](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=E8gmARGvPlI) ends.
