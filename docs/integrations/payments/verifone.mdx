Verifone provides technology for electronic payment transactions and value-added services at the point-of-sale. Sells merchant-operated, consumer-facing and self-service payment systems to the financial, retail, hospitality, petroleum, government and healthcare industries.

The integration between Verifone and Togai requires only a one-time setup. Follow the steps below:

## Install
* Navigate to the Integrations section from the left navigation bar in Togai
* Click on `Apps` to access the list of apps with integration support
* Select `Verifone` from the available apps
* Click on the `+Add connection` button 
* Provide a name for the connection and paste your:
    * `Base Url`
    * `User UID`
    * `Api key`
    * `Org ID`

### Verifone Secret Key
![](/docs/integrations/images/verifone-api-key.png)    

* Go to the Verifone merchant dashboard and click on the `Account info` tab
* Copy and paste these keys in the respective fields in Togai

## Flows

### 1. Sync accounts created from Togai to Verifone
* A new account is created in Togai
* Togai then creates a corresponding customer in Verifone and syncs the customer identifier between Togai and Verifone.
* You can see this mapping of customer identifier in the Settings tab of every customer. The setting name for Verifone customer identifier in Togai is `RazorPayCustomerId`.

### 2. Sync Togai invoices to Verifone
* A new invoice is created in Togai
* Togai then creates a corresponding payment request on Verifone using the setting `VerifoneCustomerId` for the customer on Verifone.
